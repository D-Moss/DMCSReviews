Hotel

Luxor Las Vegas

📍 Las Vegas, NV • Real Experience

An honest look at the unique pyramid architecture, the smooth inclinators, and why the beds are the real winner of the stay.

This trip took place in the post-COVID era, and I decided on a last minute upgrade to a Tower West King. The process was smooth, and it was great to see the property well-maintained and clean, even providing safety kits in the room at the time.

The bed was genuinely, remarkably comfortable—the kind of sleep that makes a trip worth it. The room was spacious and functional, stripping away the unnecassary clutter to focus on actual comfort.

One of the coolest architectural features is the inclinator-elevators that travel at a 39-degree angle. It's a smooth, efficient solution to the pyramid's unique shape that I really appreciated.

The Luxor isn't the flashiest spot in town, but it provides clarity and care where it counts. It’s honest about what it is, and there’s a real integrity in that design.
